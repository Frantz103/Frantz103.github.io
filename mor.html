Essentials of Managing

Program Objectives

By the end of Essentials of Managing, it is intended that participants will be able to:

  • Understand the role and responsibilities of a manager at MIT
  • Identify the strengths one brings to the role of manager
  • Appreciate the significance of the manager-employee relationship
  • Recognize and apply relevant law and MIT policy
  • Identify good human resources management practices and strategies to apply them
  • Find applicable MIT resources and recognize when to use them
