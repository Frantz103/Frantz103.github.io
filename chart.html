A Data Visualization Task

Daily air quality readings from pollution monitoring stations around Delhi

Please create a multi-line chart that would be suitable for publishing on our public facing website, showing the pollution trends for each station over time. The pollution variable, PM2.5 (column C), is a measure of how many small particulates are found in the air, with higher values indicating more pollution and a greater risk to human health.
